Sympathy for the Devil

Sympathy for the Devil is a rock song by the Rolling Stones. It is one of the most well-known songs by the Stones and appeared in 1968 on the album Beggars Banquet.

The song was written by the Jagger/Richards duo, but is primarily the work of Jagger. Sympathy for the Devil was recorded at Olympic Studios in London. Notably, Keith Richards plays bass on this track rather than the usual bassist Bill Wyman.
